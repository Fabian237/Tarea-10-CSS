Aligment with Gunma Prefectures

Takasaki

Takasaki is a city located in Gunma Prefecture, Japan. It is known for its Daruma dolls and hot springs.

Kusatsu

Kusatsu is a famous hot spring resort town in Gunma Prefecture, Japan. It is known for its high-quality sulfuric hot springs.

Mount Haruna

Mount Haruna is a dormant stratovolcano in Gunma Prefecture, Japan. It is a popular destination for outdoor activities and scenic views.
